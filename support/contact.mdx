We're a small team. When you contact us, you talk to a real person.

## Email

[support@shinecx.com](mailto:support@shinecx.com) — typical response within one business day.

## Phone

Call or text **(778) 977-6927** anytime during business hours (Pacific Time).

## Account deletion

To delete your account and remove all your data, see [Delete your account](/account-management/delete-account).

## Bug reports and feature requests

Send bug reports to [support@shinecx.com](mailto:support@shinecx.com) with:

- What you were trying to do
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Screenshots if possible
- The device and OS you're on (iPhone 15 / iOS 18, Pixel 8 / Android 14, etc.)

Feature requests are welcome at the same address — we read every one.
