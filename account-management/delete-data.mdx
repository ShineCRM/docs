You can delete most of your data from ShineCRM without closing your account or organization. This page covers what you can delete yourself and how to contact us for everything else.

## What you can delete yourself

The ShineCRM app and web dashboard let you delete the following directly. Each record's delete option lives on its own detail screen.

- **Contacts** — open the contact, tap the menu, choose **Delete contact**
- **Jobs** — open the job, tap the menu, choose **Delete job**
- **Estimates** — open the estimate, tap the menu, choose **Delete estimate**
- **Invoices** — open the invoice, tap the menu, choose **Delete invoice**
- **Service plans** — open the plan, tap the menu, choose **Delete plan**
- **Notes and attachments** — long-press the note or attachment, choose **Delete**
- **SMS conversations** — open the conversation, tap the menu, choose **Delete conversation**
- **Call recordings and voicemails** — open the call entry, tap the menu, choose **Delete recording**
- **Team members** — Settings, then Team, then remove the team member

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

## What you cannot delete from inside the app

A few categories of data require us to delete them on your behalf, either for legal reasons or because there is no in-app control:

- **Financial transaction records** (kept up to 7 years for tax and accounting compliance)
- **Audit and security logs** (kept up to 90 days)
- **Aggregate, anonymized usage data** (no link to your identity)

If you need any of these reviewed for deletion, email [support@shinecx.com](mailto:support@shinecx.com).

## Request deletion by email

If you would prefer us to delete data on your behalf, email [support@shinecx.com](mailto:support@shinecx.com) from the address on file with your account. Include:

- The business name and email on the account
- A description of the data you want deleted (specific records, a date range, a data category, etc.)

We will confirm receipt within one business day and complete the deletion within 14 days.

## Want to delete your whole account?

If you would rather remove your entire account and all associated data, see [Delete your account](/account-management/delete-account).
