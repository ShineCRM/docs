ShineCRM is designed to plug into the rest of your tech stack. Integrations are being built out actively; this page tracks what's available and what's planned.

## Available now

- **Stripe** for online payments via hosted checkout
- **Square** for in-person card payments via the Square Point of Sale app
- **Twilio** for SMS and voice communication
- **Resend** for sending and receiving customer email
- **Google Calendar** for two-way calendar sync

## Coming soon

- **Zapier** for connecting ShineCRM to 5,000+ other apps
- **Make** (formerly Integromat) for visual workflow automation
- **MCP servers** for AI assistant integration (Claude, ChatGPT)
- **QuickBooks** and **Xero** for accounting sync
- **Webhooks** for real-time event delivery

If there's an integration you need that isn't listed, let us know at [support@shinecx.com](mailto:support@shinecx.com).
