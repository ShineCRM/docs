ShineCRM AI is a set of features that help you run your business with less manual work. Three of them run automatically on customer activity. A fourth surface lets you connect your own Claude account to ShineCRM through MCP and operate the entire CRM by chat.

## What it does

## What ShineCRM AI does not do

ShineCRM AI never sends a message, changes a pipeline stage, schedules a job, or modifies your CRM without an approval step. Every automatic feature creates a **suggestion** that lands in your Command Centre. You review and approve or reject it.

The one exception: review request drafting after a job. Drafts land in your scheduled messages list as pending follow-ups. They wait there until their scheduled send time, and you can edit or cancel them at any point before that.

ShineCRM AI does not have access to:

- Photos or attachments
- Phone call recordings
- Other organizations' data (every query is scoped to your organization)
- Payment card details

## Privacy

Your data is processed by the Anthropic API for the duration of a single request. Under Anthropic's commercial API terms, your inputs and outputs are not used to train Anthropic's models. No customer data is shared with any other AI provider.

You can read Anthropic's [commercial terms](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/commercial-terms) and [training policy](https://privacy.claude.com/en/articles/7996868-is-my-data-used-for-model-training) directly.

## Cost and usage

Every AI call is logged with token counts and a USD cost. Owner and admin users can see total AI usage for the current month under **Settings → Usage**.
