This guide walks you through your first hour with ShineCRM. More detailed guides for each feature are coming soon.

## Sign up

Download ShineCRM from the [App Store](https://app.shinecx.com) or [Google Play](https://app.shinecx.com), or open the [web dashboard](https://app.shinecx.com) on a desktop browser.

## Create your organization

When you sign up, you'll be prompted to create an organization. This is your private workspace where contacts, jobs, invoices, and team members live. Pick a business name, an org slug (used in your customer-facing portal URL), and your local time zone.

## Add your first contact

Open the **Contacts** tab and tap **Add contact**. Enter their name, phone, email, and service address.

## Create your first job

From a contact's detail page, tap **New job**. Pick a service type, schedule a time, assign a crew member, and add a price.

## Send an estimate or invoice

After completing the job, you can convert it into an invoice or send an estimate beforehand. Customers receive a link to your hosted portal where they can view, accept, and pay.
